Description of project:
Tic-tac-toe (also known as X-O game) is a paper-and-pencil game for two players,
X and O, who take turns marking the spaces in a 3×3 grid, 4×4 grid or 5×5 grid.
The player who succeeds in placing three of their marks in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row wins the game.
